# V2 Release Definition

This document captures long-term vision features that are beyond the scope of MVP and V1.

**Goal:** Scale the platform, introduce autonomous agents, and expand the "Community Mode" capabilities of Event Memory.

## Included Capabilities
*   **Autonomous AI Agents:** Agents that negotiate meeting times or qualify leads automatically.
*   **Self-Serve Sponsorship Portal:** A dynamic ad-bidding market allowing sponsors to bid on highly specific attendee segments.
*   **Cross-Platform Identity Syndication:** Integrations with decentralized identity (DID) or LinkedIn graph sync for true global Event Memory.
*   **Enterprise Multi-Tier Organizations:** Supporting complex parent-child tenant hierarchies for massive event agencies.
