# Core Personas

This document defines the key users of the Event Intelligence Platform.

## 1. Organizer
*   **Goals:** Event execution, Revenue generation, Sponsor/Exhibitor management, Analytics.
*   **Pain Points:** [Placeholder]
*   **Success Metrics:** [Placeholder]

## 2. Attendee
*   **Goals:** Networking, Discovery, Learning, Relationship building.
*   **Pain Points:** App fatigue, losing connections post-event.
*   **Success Metrics:** Number of meaningful connections retained.

## 3. Exhibitor
*   **Goals:** Lead generation, Lead qualification, Pipeline creation.
*   **Pain Points:** ROI is hard to measure; lead capture is often manual or disconnected from CRM.
*   **Success Metrics:** Qualified leads pushed to CRM.

## 4. Sponsor
*   **Goals:** Reach, Engagement, Attribution, ROI.
*   **Pain Points:** Paying for logos rather than measurable engagement.
*   **Success Metrics:** Brand impressions, direct interactions.
