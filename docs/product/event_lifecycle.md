# Event Lifecycle

The Event Intelligence Platform creates value across three distinct phases: Before, During, and After the event. 
*The post-event experience (Event Memory) is our primary differentiator.*

The complete lifecycle modeling, including goals, pain points, AI opportunities, and revenue generation strategies, has been broken down by persona:
