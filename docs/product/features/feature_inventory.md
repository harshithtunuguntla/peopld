# Feature Inventory: Priority Lists

This document serves as the executive summary of the Feature Dictionary, grouping the highest-impact features into strategic priority lists. 

*Note: In accordance with the product strategy to prioritize quality over quantity, features that cannot be strictly justified by a persona need or revenue opportunity have been excluded. Thus, these lists represent the absolute core focus rather than bloated "Top 20" lists.*

## Top MVP Priority Features
*Focus: Proving the Event Intelligence hypothesis while delivering table-stakes operations.*
1.  **AI Copilot Lead Capture (QR Scan → Voice Note)** (Strategic Differentiator)
2.  **Basic AI Lead Prioritization (Hot/Warm/Cold)** (Strategic Differentiator)
3.  **Relationship History (People I Met)** (Strategic Moat)
4.  **Dynamic AI Matchmaking Feed** (Competitive Parity -> Differentiator)
5.  **Global Identity Resolution (Bootstrap)** (Table Stakes / Moat Enabler)

*Focus: Direct monetization and increasing SaaS LTV.*
